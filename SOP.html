LibrarySOPAbout

Purpose

How to add and maintain training modules on this GitHub Pages site.

Required fields (per module)

  • id: unique slug.
  • title, description, optional duration, tags.
  • For video, provide one of: youtubeId, loomId, loomShareUrl, or a full embedUrl.
  • Optional links: sopUrl, docUrl. Optional thumbUrl if you want a custom thumbnail.

Loom

Paste your Loom share link in loomShareUrl. We convert it to the embed URL for you. Example: "loomShareUrl": "https://www.loom.com/share/VIDEO_ID".
